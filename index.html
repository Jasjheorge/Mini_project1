H I S T O R Y
Taralets Travel Agency was built last June 07, 1997, as a travel agency that provides services through telephone and walk-in. In 2003 TTA elevated its services online to make it more convenient for its guests. They are one of the trusted agencies that provide international and domestic services with competitive promo packages. After years of serving the people, it became one of the famous travel agencies nationwide.
